Privacy Policy for Local Celebrity

Last updated: 2026-06-19

Local Celebrity ("the App") is a personal utility for remembering people and places. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

Data We Collect

The App stores the following information, entered by you:

  • Names, roles, and notes for people you add
  • Names and locations (latitude/longitude) for places you add

Location

The App uses your device's location, with your permission, only to help you drop a pin at your current location when adding a new place. Location is not collected, transmitted, or used for any purpose other than this on-device feature.

Storage and Sync

All data you enter is stored on your device and synced across your own devices using Apple's iCloud (via CloudKit), in your private iCloud database. The developer of Local Celebrity does not have access to this data, cannot view it, and does not store a copy of it on any separate server.

Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third party. The App contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children and is not directed at children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
