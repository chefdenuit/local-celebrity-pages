Frequently Asked Questions

Does my data sync across my devices?
Yes — Local Celebrity uses your private iCloud account to sync your people and places across all your devices signed into the same Apple ID. No one else can see this data.

Do I need an internet connection?
No, the app works fully offline. An internet connection is only used to sync via iCloud when available.

How do I delete a person or place?
Open a person's detail page and tap "Delete Contact," or open a place from the map and tap the trash icon to delete the place (and everyone saved there).
